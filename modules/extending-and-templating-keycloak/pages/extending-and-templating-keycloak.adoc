#  Extending and Templating Keycloak

= Extending and Templating Keycloak

Red Hat build of Keycloak provides flexibility through customization, allowing administrators to modify both the appearance and functionality of the platform.

*   **Templating:** Customizes the user-facing interfaces, including login, registration, and account management pages, to align with organizational branding.
*   **Extending:** Enhances the core functionality of the platform by implementing custom providers and features to meet specific business requirements.
